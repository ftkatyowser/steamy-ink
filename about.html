About Steamy Ink Publishing

Steamy Ink Publishing is the premier source of news and information for the Freelands, operating two distinct publications to serve the diverse interests of our readership. Founded in 1601, we have been dedicated to truth, transparency, and the occasional well-placed rumor.

Our Publications

Special Investigative Press™

A division of Steamy Ink Publishing, proudly supported by the Journalist's Guild of Civen and generously subsidized by the Breaking Bulletin Consortium. Our mission is to deliver timely, accurate, and accessible information to the Freelands community through in-depth investigative journalism.

We are the first paper to successfully implement a regional press model, meaning that while our cover pages show the global threats that unite us, our interior sections are customized to specific locations throughout the Freelands.

Steamy Ink Press™

Maplewood's Whispers, from Ear to Ink. A spotlight for Maplewood's most talked-about personalities, we are dedicated to delivering unparalleled, in-depth revelations about the captivating lives of the People of Interest who pass through our bustling town.

What are their dealings? Their true motives? Their romantic entanglements? It remains our mission to provide the perfect conversation starter for lively discussion over tea with friends.

For Non-LARP Readers

These newspapers document actual gameplay events from Kingdoms of Novitas, a live-action roleplaying game. Character relationships, political developments, and dramatic incidents all reflect real player interactions and collaborative storytelling, presented through the lens of in-world journalism.

The publications chronicle the ongoing adventures, relationships, and existential threats facing the town of Maplewood and the greater Freelands region. Each issue represents actual events that occurred during monthly game sessions, transformed into immersive fiction.

This project has been running since 2022 (in-game year 1601), with monthly issues documenting three years of collaborative storytelling.
